SampTA’s presentations and special sessions will be in the Cohen Auditorium at the Yale School of Medicine (that’s at 230 South Frontage Road). Here’s how to find the auditorium.

There are half a dozen hotels within a five-minute walk of the auditorium, all nestled around the heart of Yale’s campus.

  • The Blake Hotel (2 blocks away)
  • The New Haven Hotel (2 blocks away)
  • The Omni New Haven (4 blocks away)
  • The Study at Yale (5 blocks away)
  • The Graduate (Also 5 blocks away)
  • The Courtyard by Marriott (7 blocks away)
